The way to understand if you win is by taking the final number of each teams score at the end of each quarter. Example: End of Q1 the Score is Broncos 10 - Panthers 14, the winner will have the 0 on the Broncos line and 4 on the Panthers line. So in this case, Ian Michiels would win $25. The same rule applies for each quarter and the payouts are: Score at end of Q1 = $25 Score at Halftime end of Q2 = $50 Score at end of Q3 - $75 Final Score at end of Q4 = $100 Yes, an individual can win multiple times.
